Abstract

We introduce MedVidBench, a large-scale multi-task medical video understanding dataset with 531K video-instruction pairs covering 8 diverse tasks spanning video-level, segment-level, and frame-level understanding. This comprehensive benchmark enables systematic evaluation of medical video understanding models across heterogeneous task types.

Building on this dataset, we propose MedGRPO, a novel multi-task reinforcement learning framework specifically designed for heterogeneous medical video tasks. Our approach features cross-dataset reward normalization to ensure balanced training across different tasks and datasets, along with a Medical LLM Judge for accurate evaluation of medical video caption quality.

Compared to supervised fine-tuning (SFT) baselines, MedGRPO achieves consistent improvements across all 8 tasks, including +0.074 mIoU@0.3 on temporal action grounding and +0.588 LLM score on video summary generation.

MedVidBench Dataset

  • 531K video-instruction pairs: Large-scale dataset for medical video understanding
  • 8 diverse tasks: Covers video-level, segment-level, and frame-level understanding
  • Enables comprehensive evaluation of medical video models

MedGRPO Framework

  • Multi-Task Reinforcement Learning: Novel RL framework designed for heterogeneous medical video tasks
  • Cross-Dataset Reward Normalization: Ensures balanced training across different tasks and datasets
